## Quick Deploy Methods

### Dashboard Deployment
1. Open the Softgen project dashboard
2. Navigate to the Deployment section
3. Click "Deploy with Vercel"
4. Login to your Vercel account
5. Wait for automatic deployment (1-2 minutes)
6. Access your live project URL

### One-Click Publish
<Callout type="info">
  Simplest method for instant deployment
</Callout>

- In your project page, click the "Publish" button
- Platform handles automatic deployment
- Receive instant live URL
